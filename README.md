# Sowitech-MC-Modpack

---

## What is Sowitech?

Sowitech is the modpack which i am developing for funsies, here i will post all the progress and update it as i progress it.
## Okay what about the content?

Sowitech is not a linear modpack but rather what you would call a "Kitchen Sink", the modpack is designed around playing with friends or in servers due to the large amount of mods which differ in scope and goals, as a rule of thumb you can start with any path and not feel like you're being left behind or overlooked.

---

## Main Mods

* Create
* Immersive Engineering
* Psi
* Hexcasting
* Tetra
* Hordes
* Grimoire's of Gaia
* Progressive Difficulty
